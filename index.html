flavo

Flavo is a producer of original spice mixes based in the Netherlands. Its products are suitable for both home and professional cooking.

Brand identity and packaging focuses not on ingredients and flavors but on emotions these mixes evoke. Brand illustrations show these emotions in a playful and naive style. Color palette of each Flavo packaging is based on particular spices used for this mix.

The first tree Flavo mixes are Cosiness, Intensity and Passion.
